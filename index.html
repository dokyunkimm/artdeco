Art Deco Design is an ideas-driven architecture and interior design firm that practices at the intersection of innovation, business and behavior. With an empathetic process rooted in careful research, real-world making and the belief that all voices should be heard, we pursue solutions that foster community, encourage well-being, and enhance experience.

For more than 35 years, Art Deco Design has designed numerous and notable residential projects throughout Korea. Art Deco Design kick-started its career in 1985 by designer Kyung Sook Kim who studied design in France brought innovative value to the country. We specialize in high-end residential and branding commercial projects, with an emphasis on modern architecture.
